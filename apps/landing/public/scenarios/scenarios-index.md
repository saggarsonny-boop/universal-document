# Universal Document™ — Scenario Walkthroughs

Three stories showing what Universal Document does that PDF cannot.

---

### [The Discharge Summary](./clinical-record.md)
*A document that knows who is reading it*

A patient's discharge summary travels from hospital to pharmacist to insurer — in two languages, sealed, verified, and revoked when the medication changes. Every reader sees what they need. The correction reaches everywhere the original did.

---

### [The Lease](./contract-lifecycle.md)
*A document that proves itself*

A commercial lease is converted, negotiated, signed by both parties, notarised, and three years later produces its own proof of authenticity in a dispute — without a solicitor, without a court filing. The hash matches. The document is unchanged. The document proves itself.

---

### [The Paper](./research-paper.md)
*A document that thinks*

An academic paper is authored with queryable data tables, translated into four languages in a single file, sealed with a Bitcoin timestamp, and published with its full provenance chain embedded. An AI system anywhere in the world can read the data directly. No parser required.
